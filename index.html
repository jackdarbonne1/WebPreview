The Problem

Millions wait months for a screening that could save their life.

Bladder cancer is most treatable when caught early, yet the specialists who screen for it are concentrated in a shrinking number of places. For most patients, access depends on geography and a long wait for an appointment.

Our Approach

Enabling providers already in every community to deliver the screening patients need.

Lumenate Medical enables non-urologist providers to safely perform bladder cancer screening, with specialist oversight built in. No new urologists required.
